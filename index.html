About.

Welcome to my portfolio!

Hello, I'm Catherine Cantoran and I am a self-taught developer who loves to solve real world problems through programming. In previous non-tech management roles, I was able to solve significant problems and create inventory infrastructure through basic javascript. In between presenting to stake holders and leading a team of 10-15 people, I created a program that brought in an additional 70,000/year. Being able to solve real world issues and generating real time profit was the catalyst that started my love for programming. I then transitioned into 100Devs where I currently work on full-stack applications to help a variety of clients. In this position, I've helped lead my team using Agile methodologies while also developing, debugging and maintaining code using the MERN stack. I devote my spare time to my agency, COL + LABS where I help up-and-coming businesses establish their online presence and become fully digital. When I'm not at 100Devs or working at my agency, I love to learn new languages and new techologies through building new projects. Please take a look around at some of my projects and explore the design documentation.
